Frequently Asked Questions

How often are water rates adjusted in Connecticut? +

Water utilities in Connecticut typically file rate cases with PURA every 3-5 years. The most recent adjustments were approved in 2024 and took effect January 2025.

Which provider serves my area? +

You can check service areas on the PURA website or contact your town's public works department.

What is the average water bill in Connecticut? +

The average residential water bill ranges from $60-$120 monthly depending on usage and provider. Most households using 4,000 gallons pay $75-$90.
